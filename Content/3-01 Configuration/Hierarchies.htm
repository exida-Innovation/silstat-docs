Hierarchies

Physical

Physical

The Physical hierarchy view represents the location of each Area, Process Area, Unit, Equipment, Tag and Device onsite.

Hazards

Hazards

The Hazards hierarchy view groups items associated with a Hazard Scenario together, showing Initiating Events, Modifiers, and all Safeguards as children of a Hazard Scenario. If an Initiating Event, Modifier, or Safeguard is re-used across multiple Hazard Scenarios, it will be listed for each Hazard in the Hazards hierarchy view. The link symbol next to the name signifies that it has been reused/linked/associated in multiple areas.

Safeguards

Safeguards

The Safeguard hierarchy view groups items within a Safeguard together, showing the logical relationship between Tags. You will see the Area, Process Area, Unit, Equipment, and all Safeguards including SIFs, Alarms, Relief Devices, etc. Expanding the SIF Safeguards shows the Sensor, Logic Solver, and Final Element parts. If a Tag or Device is re-used across multiple SIFs, it will be listed for each SIF Safeguard in the Safeguard hierarchy view. The link symbol next to the name signifies that it has been reused/linked/associated in multiple areas.

Procedures

Procedures

The Procedures hierarchy view shows proof tests and procedures assigned throughout your site hierarchy. Depending on their scope, Procedures can be assigned to Units , Equipment, Safeguards, and Tags. If a Procedures is assigned to multiple Areas, it will be listed for each one. Selecting a Procedure will show you the instance of the Procedure applied to that specific part of the hierarchy.

Please Note: When building your hierarchies, you should remember that everything will be defaulted to a draft until you press Create.
