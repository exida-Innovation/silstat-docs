Landing Page

Landing Page

There are 6 main sections of information on the SILstat Landing Page.

Recent

Recent

The Recent section lists the latest items you have viewed in SILstat, ordered by the last edited. Double click on an item in the list to return to that view.

Tasks

Tasks

The Tasks section is specific to each user, showing a list of items that need your attention. Tasks can be filtered by Approvals, Procedures Upcoming, and Drafts.

Approvals

Collection events that require your review and approval are listed here. Double click on the event to view more details.

Procedures Upcoming

Scheduled proof tests and procedures with upcoming due dates are listed here. Double click on an item to initiate an event.

Drafts

Items added to the database and left in the ‘Draft’ state are listed here. The Home Page will show a maximum of 10 draft items. Select ‘View More’ to view the complete list of drafted items. Items without a Name will be given a name by default.

Insights

Insights

Insights provide at-a-glance information on the configuration of your database and event data collected. The Insights show Failures, Demands, and Recorded Procedures that can be filtered to the past 90, 180, 365, or 730 days.

You can see what Areas, Safeguards, and Devices are In-Service and Not-in-Service.

Your Organization

Your Organization

Your Organization displays company information from the licensing integration system.

Training and Help

Training and Help

Training and Help shows exida’s latest information on Training and Software Support.

Latest News

Latest News

exida News displays the latest company news.
