There are 6 main sections of information on the SILstat Landing Page.

The Dashboard Landing Page includes the following sections:

  • Recent
  • Your Organization
  • Training & Help
  • Latest News

Recent

Recent

The Recent section lists the latest items you have viewed in Dashboard, ordered by the last edited. Double click on an item in the list to return to that view.

Your Organization

Your Organization

Your Organization displays company information from the licensing integration system.

Training and Help

Training and Help shows exida’s latest information on Training and Software Support.

Latest News
