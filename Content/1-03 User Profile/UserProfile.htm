User Profile

Select your Username or Profile Picture at the top right corner of the app to view your profile. By default, your profile picture will show your initials. Once selected, you will see a menu that lists: Switch Organization, Account, Profile, and Sign Out.

Select ‘Profile’ to upload a Profile Picture and edit your First, Middle, and Last Name. You can also edit your Organization Details and Contact Information.

Within Preferences, you can adjust your Time Zone and Country / Region. You can also select your preferred Language, Date format, Time format, Unit of Measurements, and Theme.
